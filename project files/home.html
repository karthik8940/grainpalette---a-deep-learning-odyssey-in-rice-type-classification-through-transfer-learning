About Us

GrainPalette is a deep learning-powered rice classification platform. It uses advanced image recognition models to accurately detect rice types such as Basmati, Jasmine, Arborio, Ipsala, and Karacadag.

This project was developed by sama venkata sai karthik and team as part of a research initiative on agricultural AI applications.
